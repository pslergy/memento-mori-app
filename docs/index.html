How It Works

ü¶á

Acoustic Sonar

Air-gapped discovery using ultrasonic BFSK (18‚Äì20 kHz). Works in radio silence or RF-monitored zones. No pairing required ‚Äî devices find each other by sound.

üì°

BLE Control Plane

Zero-connect topology: nodes broadcast gradient state in advertising packets. No heavy connections ‚Äî just lightweight discovery and hop-count inference.

‚ö°

Wi-Fi Direct Burst

When a BLE link is verified, a high-bandwidth Wi-Fi group forms to flush the message queue. Biological burst-mode saves up to 90% battery.

üëª

Ghost Identity

Fully functional offline identity (Ed25519) in SQLite. When the internet returns, your ghost merges atomically with a persistent ID ‚Äî no history loss.
